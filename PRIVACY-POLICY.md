# Privacy Policy — Approach

_Last updated: [ADD DATE BEFORE PUBLISHING]_

**Approach** ("the app") is a single-player, offline air-traffic-control game.

## Data we collect

**None.** The app does not collect, store off your device, transmit, or share any
personal or sensitive user data. Specifically:

- The app requests **no Android permissions** (no internet, location, contacts,
  storage, camera, microphone, or device identifiers).
- The app contains **no advertising, analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs**.
- The app makes **no network requests** — it runs entirely from files bundled
  inside the app and never contacts any server.

## Data stored on your device

Your game progress, settings, statistics, and best score are saved **locally on
your own device only** (in the app's private storage). This information never
leaves your device, is not accessible to us or anyone else, and is removed if you
clear the app's data or uninstall the app.

## Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be provided with the app and the
"Last updated" date above will be revised.
