APPROACH CONTROL

Tower / Turn-Based ATC Simulation

You are the controller. Aircraft arrive low on fuel and departures wait at the gates. Every turn you issue runway clearances, then hit EXECUTE to advance the clock. Keep the metal moving — and never let two aircraft meet.

  • Select a flight strip → choose a runway button (or HOLD).
  • Arrivals burn 1 fuel per turn — the gauge reads how many turns of fuel remain. Hit zero in the air = crash + a strike.
  • One aircraft per runway per turn. 14/32 physically crosses 27L/09R — you can't run both at once.
  • Heavies (widebodies) need a long runway — you now have two (27R/09L and 27C/09C) — and leave wake turbulence, blocking that runway the next turn. The Superjumbo blocks it for two.
  • Watch for weather closures and fuel / medical emergencies as levels climb.
  • Every wave is always landable with the right order — the pressure is finding it before fuel runs out.
  • You start with one runway. Each operation earns funds — tap ✦ BUILD to open new runways, extend them for heavies, add rapid-exit taxiways, ILS, terminals and more. More capacity means more traffic and more income.
  • Three strikes and you're relieved of duty. Your shift auto-saves each turn.
